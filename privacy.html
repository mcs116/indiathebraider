Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

India the Braider LLC ("India the Braider," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we share it, and your rights regarding your personal data. By using our website or services, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

India the Braider LLC may collect the following categories of information:

a. Information You Provide Directly

  • Full name (first and last)
  • Email address
  • Phone number (including mobile number for SMS communications)
  • Messages, inquiries, and communications submitted through our contact form
  • SMS opt-in consent preferences and records
  • Appointment booking details (date, time, service type)

b. Information Collected Automatically

  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Browser type, version, and operating system
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)
  • Pages visited, time spent on pages, and navigation paths
  • Referring website or source
  • Date and time of each visit

c. Cookies, Tracking Technologies & Analytics

India the Braider LLC uses cookies, pixel tags, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to improve your browsing experience, analyze website traffic, and understand how visitors interact with our site. We may use third-party analytics services, including but not limited to Google Analytics, to collect and analyze usage data such as pages viewed, session duration, bounce rate, and traffic sources. These analytics services may use cookies and similar technologies to gather information about your use of our website. Analytics data is used solely to improve our website and services and is not used to personally identify individual visitors. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies, delete existing cookies, or alert you when a cookie is being set. Disabling cookies may limit some features of our website. For more information about how Google Analytics collects and processes data, visit Google's privacy documentation.

2. How We Use Your Information

India the Braider LLC uses the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries, questions, and service requests
  • To schedule, confirm, and manage appointments
  • To send transactional SMS messages (appointment confirmations, service updates, inquiry responses) if you have explicitly opted in
  • To send promotional SMS messages (new braid styles, hair care tips, special offers, seasonal trends) if you have separately and explicitly opted in
  • To improve our website, services, and customer experience
  • To analyze website traffic and usage patterns via cookies and analytics tools
  • To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal obligations
  • To protect against fraud, unauthorized access, and other security threats

3. SMS Messaging & Mobile Information

When you provide your mobile phone number and opt in to receive SMS messages from India the Braider LLC, the following applies:

  • Message types: Transactional messages (service updates, appointment confirmations, inquiry responses) and/or promotional messages (offers, new styles, hair care tips), depending on your selected consent preferences.
  • Message frequency: Message frequency may vary, up to 4 messages per month.
  • Opt-out: You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any SMS message from India the Braider LLC. You will receive a confirmation and will no longer receive messages unless you re-subscribe.
  • Help: Reply HELP to any message for assistance.
  • Costs: Message and data rates may apply. Standard text messaging charges from your wireless carrier will apply. Contact your carrier for plan details.
  • Consent: Consent to receive SMS messages is entirely optional and is not required to purchase any goods or services from India the Braider LLC.
CRITICAL — No Sharing of SMS Opt-In Data: No mobile information will be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes. Information sharing to subcontractors in support services, such as customer service, is permitted. India the Braider LLC does not sell, rent, lease, trade, or otherwise disclose your mobile phone number, SMS consent data, or any information collected through SMS opt-in to any third parties for marketing, advertising, or promotional purposes. Your SMS opt-in information is used solely by India the Braider LLC for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy and our SMS Terms & Conditions. All mobile information and consent data will remain strictly confidential.

4. Information Sharing & Disclosure

India the Braider LLC does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers & subcontractors: We may share information with trusted third-party service providers and subcontractors who assist us in operating our business and providing support services such as customer service, website hosting, appointment scheduling platforms, and SMS messaging infrastructure, provided they agree to keep your information confidential and use it only for the services they provide to us.
  • Legal compliance: We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
  • Safety & protection: We may disclose information to protect the rights, property, or safety of India the Braider LLC, our clients, or the public.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes.

5. Data Security

India the Braider LLC implements reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include but are not limited to: encrypted data transmission (SSL/TLS) on our website, secure storage of consent records and personal data, access controls limiting employee and contractor access to personal information on a need-to-know basis, regular review of data collection and storage practices, and use of trusted third-party service providers who maintain their own security standards. While we strive to protect your information using commercially reasonable methods, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. SMS consent records are retained for the duration of your subscription and for a reasonable period thereafter for compliance purposes.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You may request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You may request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: You may request that we delete your personal information, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Opt-out of SMS: You may opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP to any message.
  • Opt-out of marketing emails: You may unsubscribe from marketing emails using the unsubscribe link provided in each email.
  • Update preferences: You may update your communication preferences by contacting us directly.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact India the Braider LLC through our website contact form.

8. Children's Privacy

If we learn that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will promptly delete it. If you believe a child under 18 has opted in to SMS messaging, please contact us immediately.
